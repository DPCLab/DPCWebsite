Russian Meddling

A four-part series exploring Russian meddling on American social media.Miles McCain and Jeffrey Shen

The Trolls Are Among Us

Russian Meddling Pt. 1 • Oct. 29, 2018

Much remains unknown about the Russian effort to use social media to influence the 2016 American election. We do know, however, that the disinformation campaign respected no boundaries: it promoted both conservative and liberal messaging, reached nearly a majority of Americans, and incited distrust in the American political system.

Inside the Mind of the Troll

Russian Meddling Pt. 2 • Nov. 7, 2018

We've released an interactive machine learning model that helps make the Russian disinformation campaign just slightly more accessible. Given any English Tweet, our model determines whether that Tweet uses language specifically popular among Russian trolls—or whether the Tweet more closely resembles organic content.

Interactive Explorer

Russian Meddling Pt. 4 • NOV. 13, 2018

We used machine learning to identify patterns of language in Russian government trolls. Use this interactive online tool to learn more about the ways that Russian state-sponsored government trolls post online—and how it compares to your own language.
